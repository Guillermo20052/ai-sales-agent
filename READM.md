# AI Sales Agent

Production-oriented AI SaaS platform focused on automating customer interaction, lead capture, and business workflows through LLM-powered agents and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG).

Built as a full-stack AI engineering project to explore scalable AI systems, workflow automation, semantic retrieval, and real-world SaaS infrastructure.

---

## Overview

AI Sales Agent is designed to function as an intelligent business assistant capable of:

- answering customer questions,
- retrieving contextual business information,
- capturing leads,
- guiding users through services/products,
- and automating customer interaction workflows.

The platform uses modern AI engineering practices including:
- LLM orchestration,
- semantic search,
- vector-based retrieval,
- automated web crawling,
- and AI-assisted development workflows.
